What We Do

Services Built Around Real Problems

From strategy to implementation, Sentinel Ridge delivers across the full spectrum of technology consulting — combining operational experience with technical depth to solve problems that matter to your business.

01
Technology Consulting

Strategic technology advisory for organizations navigating modernization, vendor selection, architecture decisions, and digital transformation. We translate complex technical landscapes into clear, actionable roadmaps.

02
Operational Analytics

Turn fragmented operational data into decision-ready insight. We design and implement analytics pipelines, dashboards, and reporting systems that surface what's actually driving performance — and what's holding it back.

03
Software Development

Custom software built to fit your operations — not the other way around. From internal tools and process automation to full-scale applications, we deliver production-ready software with a focus on maintainability and performance.

04
Logistics Optimization

Deep expertise in supply chain and logistics operations — from network design and inventory modeling to route optimization and vendor performance analysis. We find efficiency where it counts most.

05
Management Consulting

Organizational strategy, process improvement, and change management grounded in operational reality. We help leadership teams align on priorities, restructure workflows, and execute decisions with confidence.

06
Government Contracting & SBIR

For companies pursuing federal contracts or SBIR funding, we provide topic analysis, proposal strategy, and compliance guidance — leveraging our operational background to build proposals that are technically credible and competitively positioned.
